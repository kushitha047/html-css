Wings of Fire

by Arun Tiwari

"Wings of Fire" is the autobiography of A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, former President of India, co-authored with Arun Tiwari. It chronicles his life journey from a humble childhood in Rameswaram to becoming a renowned scientist and then President. The book highlights his dedication, simple living, and the importance of family and community in shaping his life. It also details his contributions to India's space and missile programs. The story emphasizes the role of hard work and perseverance in achieving success
